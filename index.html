About MamoPedia AI

MamoPedia AI is a friendly, chat-based learning companion designed specifically for Iraqi students. Powered by AI and a tailored curriculum, it offers personalized homework support, problem-solving, and quizzes - all in Arabic, Kurdish, and English. Whether a student is revising for an exam, stuck on a tricky problem, or looking for extra practice, MamoPedia AI is there to help, anytime and anywhere.

  • Chat-based, conversational learning.
  • Built for Iraqi students and their local curriculum.
  • Full support for Arabic, Kurdish, and English.
