FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Short answers to the things parents and students ask us most.

Which subjects and grade levels does MamoPedia AI cover? expand_more
MamoPedia AI supports core subjects taught in Iraqi primary and secondary schools, including mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, Arabic, English, and social studies. Coverage expands continuously based on pilot feedback.
Which languages can my child use to ask questions? expand_more
Students can chat in Arabic, Kurdish (Sorani), or English. MamoPedia AI automatically detects the language of each question and responds in the same language.
Is MamoPedia AI free to use? expand_more
Yes. During our pilot program the core experience is free. We'll announce any paid plans well in advance and always keep a free tier available for students.
How do you protect student privacy and data? expand_more
We minimize what we collect and never sell student data. Conversations are used to improve learning recommendations and are protected with modern encryption. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What ages is MamoPedia AI designed for? expand_more
The experience is tuned for students roughly ages 10-18 and adapts its tone and difficulty based on the grade level you set. Younger children should use it with adult supervision.
Does it work offline? expand_more
MamoPedia AI currently needs an internet connection to generate personalized answers and quizzes. We're exploring a lightweight offline study pack for areas with limited connectivity.
