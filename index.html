About Mamopedia.

Mamopedia is a versatile Q&A platform and mobile app tailored to the needs of students, teachers, and educational institutions. Our mission is to harness the power of social media for education. Published in 2023 in Iraq by a team of educators, Mamopedia was created to fill the gap in Middle Eastern society's education sector by using cutting-edge EdTech. This innovative Q&A platform connects students and educators, facilitating collaborative learning, resource sharing, and dynamic discussions. By leveraging technology to bridge the educational divide, Mamopedia aspires to empower individuals and institutions across the region, contributing to the advancement of education in the Middle East.
